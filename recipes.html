Recipe For Lasagna

What lasagna is a how to make it!

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that has gained worldwide popularity for its rich and comforting flavors. It is a layered pasta dish made with flat rectangular sheets of pasta, typically alternating with layers of meat sauce, cheese, and various fillings. The pasta sheets are typically boiled until al dente before being assembled with the other ingredients. The meat sauce, often made with ground beef, onions, garlic, and tomatoes, is cooked to a thick and savory consistency. Generous amounts of cheese, such as mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan, are interspersed between the layers, adding a creamy and indulgent element to the dish. Once assembled, the lasagna is baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the flavors meld together to create a hearty and satisfying meal.

Making lasagna from scratch is a labor of love that rewards with a truly delicious and comforting meal. To prepare a classic meat lasagna, begin by browning ground beef in a large skillet along with onions and garlic until cooked through and fragrant. Add tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, herbs like basil and oregano, salt, and pepper to create a rich and flavorful meat sauce. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, eggs, grated Parmesan, and chopped fresh parsley to make the cheese mixture. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish, followed by a layer of uncooked lasagna noodles. Add a layer of the cheese mixture and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella. Repeat these layers until you run out of ingredients, ensuring the final layer is cheese and mozzarella. Bake the lasagna in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 30-40 minutes, until the cheese is golden and bubbly. Allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving, and enjoy the comforting layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese that make lasagna an all-time favorite dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 24 ounces tomato sauce
  • 14 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles(uncooked)
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
